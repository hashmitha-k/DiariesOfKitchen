Potato Wedges

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, cut into wedges
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary (optional)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Prepare Wedges: Wash and cut potatoes into wedges, leaving the skin on.
  3. Season: In a large bowl, toss the wedges with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Arrange on Tray: Spread the wedges in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake: Bake for 35-40 minutes, flipping halfway through, until crispy and golden brown.
  6. Optional: Sprinkle with Parmesan and rosemary during the last 5 minutes of baking.
  7. Serve: Enjoy hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Diet Plan & Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 cup of potato wedges

  • Calories: 200 kcal
