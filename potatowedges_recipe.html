Potato Wedges

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper for easy cleanup.
  2. Prepare Potatoes: Wash the potatoes thoroughly and cut them into wedges. Leave the skin on for extra texture.
  3. Season: In a large bowl, toss the potato wedges with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, oregano, chili powder (if using), salt, and pepper until well-coated.
  4. Arrange on Baking Sheet: Place the seasoned potato wedges in a single layer on the baking sheet. Ensure they are evenly spaced out for even cooking.
  5. Bake: Bake for 30-35 minutes, flipping the wedges halfway through cooking, until they are golden and crispy on the outside and tender inside.
  6. Serve: Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce (e.g., ketchup, ranch, or garlic mayo).
