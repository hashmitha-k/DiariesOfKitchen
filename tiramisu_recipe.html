Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 1 package of ladyfinger biscuits (about 24 biscuits)
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee (cooled)
  • 1 tablespoon rum (optional)
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mascarpone cheese
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Coffee: Brew the coffee and let it cool. Add rum if using.
  2. Whisk the Egg Yolks: In a heatproof bowl, whisk the egg yolks with sugar. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water and whisk until the mixture becomes thick and pale.
  3. Whip the Cream: In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  4. Combine Mascarpone and Egg Mixture: Gently fold mascarpone cheese into the egg mixture.
  5. Layer the Tiramisu: Dip the ladyfinger biscuits in the cooled coffee for a second or two. Lay them at the bottom of a serving dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the biscuits.
  6. Repeat Layers: Repeat the process with another layer of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  7. Chill the Tiramisu: Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Serve: Before serving, dust the top of the tiramisu with cocoa powder and serve chilled.

Diet Plan & Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 serving (1 slice)

  • Calories: 350 kcal
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 33g
  • Protein: 5g
  • Sugar: 22g

Diet Tips:

  • For a lighter version: Use reduced-fat mascarpone and whipped topping.
  • For a non-alcoholic version: Omit the rum and use just coffee.

Dietary Information

  • Gluten-Free: This recipe is not gluten-free due to the ladyfingers used.
  • Vegetarian: Yes, this recipe is vegetarian-friendly.
  • Contains Dairy: Yes, it contains mascarpone cheese and heavy cream.
  • Contains Eggs: Yes, this recipe contains eggs (egg yolks).
  • Contains Alcohol: Optional rum is used in the recipe.
