Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 1 package (7 oz) ladyfinger cookies
  • 2 cups mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Coffee Mixture: In a shallow dish, combine the brewed coffee and coffee liqueur. Set aside.
  2. Whip Cream: In a large bowl, beat heavy cream and powdered sugar until stiff peaks form.
  3. Prepare Mascarpone Filling: In another bowl, whisk together mascarpone cheese and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Combine: Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined.
  5. Assemble Tiramisu: Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture (donâ€™t soak). Layer them in the bottom of a 9x9-inch dish. Spread half of the mascarpone filling over the ladyfingers. Repeat the layers with remaining ladyfingers and mascarpone filling.
  6. Chill: Cover and refrigerate the tiramisu for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.
  7. Finish: Just before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder and sprinkle with chocolate shavings, if desired.
