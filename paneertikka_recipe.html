Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Onion rings for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade: In a mixing bowl, combine 1/4 cup of yogurt, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 tablespoon red chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 tablespoon garam masala, 1 tablespoon coriander powder, salt, and 2 tablespoons of oil. Mix well.
  2. Marinate the Paneer: Add the paneer cubes to the marinade and mix gently. Ensure that all pieces are coated well. Cover the bowl and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes or longer for more flavor.
  3. Grill the Paneer: Preheat a grill or a grill pan. Once hot, thread the marinated paneer cubes onto skewers and grill them for about 10-12 minutes, turning them halfway through. You can also bake the paneer in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and onion rings. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 200 kcal per serving
  • Fat: 14g
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Gluten-Free: Yes
  • Vegetarian: Yes
  • Servings: 4
