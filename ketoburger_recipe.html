Keto Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 large lettuce leaves (for buns)
  • 4 slices cheese (cheddar or Swiss)
  • 1 small avocado, sliced
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon pickles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Patties: In a bowl, mix ground beef, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Shape into 4 burger patties.
  2. Cook Patties: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side or until desired doneness.
  3. Assemble Burger: Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom, top with the cooked patty, cheese slice, avocado, tomato, and mayonnaise. Add mustard and pickles if desired.
  4. Serve: Place another lettuce leaf on top to close the burger. Serve immediately and enjoy!
