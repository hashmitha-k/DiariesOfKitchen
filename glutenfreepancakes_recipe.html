Gluten-Free Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder (ensure it's gluten-free)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any dairy-free milk)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Fresh fruit and maple syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together gluten-free flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together almond milk, egg, melted butter, vanilla extract, and apple cider vinegar (if using).
  3. Combine: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. The batter will be slightly thick, but if it's too thick, add a little more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Cook Pancakes: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Serve your pancakes hot with fresh fruit and maple syrup. Enjoy!
