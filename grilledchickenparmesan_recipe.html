Grilled Chicken Parmesan

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season chicken breasts with olive oil, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked.
  4. Top each breast with marinara sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Close the grill lid until cheese melts.
  5. Serve hot with a side of salad or pasta.

Diet Plan & Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 chicken breast

  • Calories: 320 kcal
  • Fat: 14g
  • Carbohydrates: 8g
  • Protein: 40g
