Vegan Tacos

Ingredients

  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked black beans
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Hot sauce or salsa for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the Veggies: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add sliced onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the corn and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Season with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Warm the Tortillas: Heat the corn tortillas on a dry skillet or grill for 1-2 minutes per side until warm and slightly charred.
  3. Assemble the Tacos: Place warm tortillas on a plate and top with black beans, corn mixture, avocado slices, red onions, and cilantro. Squeeze lime juice on top.
  4. Optional: Add hot sauce or salsa for an extra kick!
  5. Serve: Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious vegan tacos!

Diet Plan & Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 taco

  • Calories: 150 kcal
  • Fat: 7g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
  • Protein: 4g
  • Sugar: 2g

Diet Tips:

  • For a low-fat version: Use fewer avocado slices or omit the oil in cooking.
  • For a gluten-free version: Ensure the tortillas are gluten-free.
