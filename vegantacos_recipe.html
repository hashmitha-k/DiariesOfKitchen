Vegan Tacos

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked black beans (or refried beans)
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 1/4 cup salsa (optional)
  • 1/4 cup vegan sour cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Filling: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add black beans, cumin, chili powder, and garlic powder. Stir to combine and cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  2. Warm Tortillas: Heat the tortillas in a separate pan for about 1 minute on each side until soft and warm.
  3. Assemble Tacos: Place warm tortillas on a plate. Add a spoonful of the black bean mixture in the center of each tortilla.
  4. Add Toppings: Top with diced tomatoes, avocado slices, shredded lettuce, red onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime. Optionally, add salsa and vegan sour cream for extra flavor.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious vegan tacos!
