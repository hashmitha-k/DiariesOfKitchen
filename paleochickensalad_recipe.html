Paleo Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast (shredded or diced)
  • 1/2 cup homemade or paleo-approved mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup apple, diced (optional for sweetness)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter lettuce leaves (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Chicken: Cook and shred or dice the chicken breast if not already done.
  2. Mix Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine chicken, mayonnaise, celery, red onion, apple (if using), and parsley.
  3. Season: Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Stir until everything is well mixed.
  4. Chill (Optional): For better flavor, refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve: Spoon chicken salad onto butter lettuce leaves and enjoy!

Diet Plan & Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 cup of chicken salad

  • Calories: 320 kcal
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbohydrates: 5g
  • Protein: 28g
  • Sugar: 2g

Dietary Information

  • Gluten-Free: Yes
  • Vegetarian: No (contains chicken)
  • Contains Dairy: No
  • Contains Eggs: Yes (in mayonnaise)
  • Paleo: Yes
