Paleo Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil mayonnaise (Paleo-friendly)
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (ensure it's Paleo-friendly)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (optional)
  • Mixed greens or lettuce for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Chicken: Shred the cooked chicken breast into bite-sized pieces. Place in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Combine Veggies: Add the chopped celery, red onion, and parsley to the bowl with chicken. Stir to combine.
  3. Make Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, and apple cider vinegar (optional). Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Mix Salad: Pour the dressing over the chicken and veggie mixture. Toss everything together until well coated.
  5. Garnish: Top with sliced almonds and additional parsley for garnish if desired. Serve on a bed of mixed greens or lettuce, if using.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately or refrigerate for later. Enjoy your healthy, Paleo Chicken Salad!
