Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/4 cup Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, for cooking

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. Cook the Pancetta: In a large pan, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the pancetta or guanciale and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan, and Pecorino cheeses, and a pinch of pepper until smooth.
  4. Combine Pasta and Pancetta: Add the drained spaghetti to the pan with pancetta, tossing to coat in the rendered fat. Let the pasta cool for a minute, so the eggs donâ€™t scramble when added.
  5. Make Carbonara Sauce: Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta and quickly toss to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time until the desired sauce consistency is achieved.
  6. Serve: Season with additional pepper and Parmesan to taste. Serve immediately.
