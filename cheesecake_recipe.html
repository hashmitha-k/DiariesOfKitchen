Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust: Preheat oven to 325째F (163째C). In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then let it cool.
  2. Prepare the Filling: Beat softened cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add vanilla and eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust.
  3. Bake the Cheesecake: Bake at 325째F (163째C) for 55-60 minutes, or until the center is almost set. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside for 1 hour.
  4. Cool the Cheesecake: Let the cheesecake cool at room temperature for 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Prepare the Topping: Mix sour cream, sugar, and vanilla. Spread the sour cream mixture over the chilled cheesecake.
  6. Serve: Remove cheesecake from the springform pan, slice, and serve! Enjoy!
