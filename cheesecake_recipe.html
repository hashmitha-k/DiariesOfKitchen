Cheesecake

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust: Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). In a mixing bowl, combine 1 and 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs with 1/4 cup of sugar. Add 1/2 cup of melted butter and stir until the crumbs are evenly moistened. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes and set aside to cool.
  2. Make the Filling: In a large bowl, beat 3 packages (8 oz each) of softened cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add 1 cup of sugar and continue beating until creamy. Mix in 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. Add 3 large eggs one at a time, mixing well after each. Finally, blend in 1 cup sour cream until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Bake the Cheesecake: Pour the filling over the crust in the springform pan. Tap the pan lightly to remove air bubbles. Place the pan on a baking sheet and bake at 325°F (163°C) for 55-60 minutes, or until the center is set and the top is lightly golden.
  4. Cool Gradually: Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the oven door slightly open for 1 hour to prevent cracking.
  5. Chill: Let the cheesecake cool at room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for best results.
  6. Serve: Once chilled, remove from the springform pan. Top with fruit, whipped cream, or enjoy it plain!

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 320 kcal per serving
  • Fat: 25g
  • Protein: 6g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Gluten-Free: Yes (if using gluten-free graham crackers)
  • Dairy-Free: Can be made dairy-free (use dairy-free cream cheese and sour cream)
  • Servings: 8
