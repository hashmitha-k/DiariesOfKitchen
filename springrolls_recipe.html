Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • 12 rice paper wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/2 cup cooked shrimp or tofu (optional)
  • 1/2 cup rice vermicelli noodles (cooked)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce or peanut sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Prepare all the ingredients by cutting vegetables into thin strips and cooking vermicelli noodles.
  2. Fill a large shallow dish with warm water. Dip one rice paper wrapper into the water for about 5 seconds until softened.
  3. Lay the softened wrapper on a clean, damp towel.
  4. Place a small portion of each ingredient in the center of the wrapper.
  5. Fold the sides over the filling and roll tightly from the bottom up.
  6. Repeat for the remaining wrappers and ingredients.
  7. Serve with soy sauce or peanut sauce for dipping. Enjoy!
