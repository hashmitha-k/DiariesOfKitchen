Honey Glazed Salmon Recipe

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix honey, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic, and lemon juice.
  2. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and add a little oil.
  4. Place salmon fillets skin-side down and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Flip salmon and pour honey glaze over the fillets. Cook for another 3-4 minutes, spooning sauce over as it thickens.
  6. Remove from heat once salmon is cooked through and glaze is sticky.
  7. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Diet Note

This honey glazed salmon is high in protein and omega-3s, perfect for a healthy and flavorful meal. Pairs well with steamed veggies or rice.
