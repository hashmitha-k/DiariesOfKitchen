Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup spinach (optional)
  • 1 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds or flaxseeds (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for desired thickness)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Peel the banana, wash the strawberries and blueberries, and gather any optional ingredients (like spinach or seeds).
  2. Blend: Add the banana, strawberries, blueberries, spinach (if using), Greek yogurt, honey, and almond milk into a blender.
  3. Blend Until Smooth: Blend on high until smooth. If you prefer a thicker smoothie, add some ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve: Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately! Optionally, garnish with a few extra berries or a sprinkle of seeds.

Diet Plan & Nutritional Information

Serving Size: 1 glass

  • Calories: 200 kcal
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Protein: 6g
  • Sugar: 15g

Diet Tips:

  • For a protein boost: Add a scoop of protein powder or use a high-protein yogurt.
  • For a lower-calorie version: Use a low-fat yogurt and skip the honey.

Dietary Information

  • Gluten-Free: Yes
  • Vegetarian: Yes
  • Vegan: Yes (if using plant-based yogurt and non-dairy milk)
  • Contains Dairy: Yes (if using Greek yogurt)
  • Contains Nuts: Yes (if using almond milk)
