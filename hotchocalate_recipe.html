Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional, for extra richness)
  • Whipped cream (for topping, optional)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the Milk: In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until hot, but not boiling.
  2. Mix Cocoa and Sugar: In a separate bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder and sugar.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Add the cocoa mixture to the hot milk, stirring until the cocoa and sugar are completely dissolved.
  4. Add Flavor: Stir in the vanilla extract and heavy cream (if using) for extra richness.
  5. Serve: Pour the hot chocolate into a mug and top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings or cocoa powder if desired.
