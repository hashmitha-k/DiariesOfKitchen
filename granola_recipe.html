Granola Bars

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter or almond butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries or raisins (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or cashews) (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pan: Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a small saucepan, melt honey, peanut butter (or almond butter), coconut oil, brown sugar, and vanilla extract over medium heat. Stir until smooth and fully combined.
  3. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, salt, chocolate chips, dried cranberries, and nuts.
  4. Mix Wet & Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet mixture over the dry ingredients. Stir until everything is well coated.
  5. Press into Pan: Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking pan and press it down firmly to compact the mixture evenly.
  6. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until the bars have set and firmed up.
  7. Slice & Serve: Once set, remove the granola from the pan and slice into bars. Store in an airtight container for up to a week!

Diet Information

Calories: 150 kcal per bar

Diet Type: Gluten-Free, Vegan, High-Energy

Suitable For: Energy Boost, Healthy Snacks
