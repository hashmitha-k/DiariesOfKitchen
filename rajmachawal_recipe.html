Rajma Chawal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kidney beans (rajma), soaked overnight
  • 2 cups cooked basmati rice
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil Rajma: Pressure cook soaked rajma with salt and 3 cups of water for 4-5 whistles or until soft. Set aside.
  2. Prepare Masala: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Then add turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the spices are aromatic.
  4. Add Tomato Puree: Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates from the masala.
  5. Add Rajma: Add the boiled rajma with some of its stock. Simmer for 15–20 minutes for flavors to blend. Mash a few beans to thicken the gravy.
  6. Serve: Garnish with coriander and serve hot with steamed rice.

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 280 kcal per serving
  • Protein: 10g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
  • Vegan: Yes
  • Gluten-Free: Yes
  • Servings: 4
