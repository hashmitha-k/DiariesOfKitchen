Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 12 large white mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Mushrooms: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Clean mushrooms and remove stems.
  2. Make the Filling: In a bowl, mix breadcrumbs, cream cheese, Parmesan, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stuff the Mushrooms: Fill each mushroom cap with the mixture, pressing gently.
  4. Bake: Place stuffed mushrooms on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and bake for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve: Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
