Mint Mojito

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 oz white rum
  • Club soda or sparkling water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime slices (for garnish, optional)
  • Mint sprig (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

  1. Muddle the Mint: In a tall glass, place the mint leaves, lime wedges, and sugar. Use a muddler or the back of a spoon to gently crush the mint and lime to release the oils and juice.
  2. Add the Rum: Pour the white rum into the glass and stir to combine with the muddled mint and lime.
  3. Fill with Ice: Add ice cubes to the glass, then top with club soda or sparkling water, depending on your preference for fizz.
  4. Garnish and Serve: Stir the mojito and garnish with a lime slice and a mint sprig. Serve chilled.
