Nachos

Ingredients

  • 1 large bag of tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup jalapeños (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup black olives (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup green onions (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup ground beef or chicken (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Layer the Chips: Spread the tortilla chips evenly on a baking sheet or oven-safe dish.
  3. Add Cheese: Sprinkle shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheese evenly over the chips.
  4. Optional Toppings: Add your optional toppings, such as ground beef or chicken (pre-cooked), jalapeños, olives, and diced tomatoes.
  5. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and the chips are golden brown.
  6. Serve: Remove from the oven and top with chopped green onions, sour cream, and salsa. Serve hot and enjoy!
