Nachos

Ingredients (Classic Nachos)

  • 1 large bag of tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup jalapeños (sliced)

Ingredients (Thin Crust Nachos)

  • 1 large bag of thin tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/3 cup guacamole
  • 1/3 cup fresh pico de gallo

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Spread tortilla chips evenly on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle cheese and optional toppings.
  4. Bake for 8-10 minutes until cheese melts.
  5. Remove from oven, garnish, and serve hot!
