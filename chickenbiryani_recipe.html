Chicken Biryani

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Fresh cilantro & mint leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil or ghee
  • 2.5 cups water or chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Rice: Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak it for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Sauté the Onions: Heat oil or ghee in a large pan. Add bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon stick. Then, add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Cook the Chicken: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Add chicken pieces, turmeric, red chili powder, and biryani masala. Stir well and cook until chicken is browned.
  4. Add Tomatoes and Yogurt: Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add yogurt and mix well.
  5. Layer the Rice: Spread the soaked rice evenly over the chicken mixture. Pour in 2.5 cups of water or broth. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle with chopped cilantro and mint. Serve hot with raita.

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 350 kcal per serving
  • Fat: 15g
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Gluten-Free: Yes
  • Dairy-Free: Can be made dairy-free (omit yogurt or use dairy-free yogurt)
  • Servings: 4
