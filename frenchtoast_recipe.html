Classic French Toast

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Maple syrup (for serving)
  • Fresh berries (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the egg mixture: In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Heat the pan: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and add butter to coat the surface.
  3. Dip the bread: Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are coated.
  4. Cook the toast: Place the coated bread onto the skillet and cook for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Serve the French toast with maple syrup and fresh berries (optional).

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 250 kcal per serving
  • Protein: 8g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 32g
  • Vegetarian: Yes
  • Gluten-Free: No (unless using gluten-free bread)
  • Servings: 2
