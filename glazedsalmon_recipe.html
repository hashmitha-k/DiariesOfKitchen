Honey Glazed Salmon

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Prepare the glaze: In a small bowl, combine honey, soy sauce, olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Glaze the salmon: Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet and brush them with the glaze mixture.
  4. Bake the salmon: Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and flaky.
  5. Serve: Serve the salmon with lemon wedges and your favorite side dishes.

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 350 kcal per serving
  • Protein: 25g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Non-Vegan: No
  • Gluten-Free: Yes
  • Servings: 2
