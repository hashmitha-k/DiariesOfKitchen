Mozzarella Sticks

Ingredients

  • 12 mozzarella cheese sticks
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Marinara sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Prepare the breading stations: flour, egg mixture, and seasoned breadcrumbs.
  2. Coat mozzarella sticks thoroughly and double-coat for extra crispiness.
  3. Freeze for at least 1 hour to prevent cheese from melting during frying.
  4. Fry in 350°F (175°C) oil until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve hot with marinara sauce for dipping.
