Recipe Details

Mozzarella Sticks

Ingredients

  • 12 mozzarella cheese sticks
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Marinara sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Breading: Set up three shallow bowls. Place flour in the first, whisk eggs and milk in the second, and mix breadcrumbs, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in the third.
  2. Coat the Mozzarella Sticks: Dredge each cheese stick in flour, then dip in the egg mixture, and finally coat with the seasoned breadcrumbs. Repeat for a double coating.
  3. Freeze the Sticks: Place coated mozzarella sticks on a tray and freeze for at least 1 hour to prevent melting while frying.
  4. Fry the Mozzarella Sticks: Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C). Fry sticks in batches for 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Drain and Serve: Remove sticks from oil and place on a paper towel. Serve hot with marinara sauce.
