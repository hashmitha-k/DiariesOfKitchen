Recipe Details

Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Optional: chocolate chips, fruit, or flavorings of choice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Mixture: In a large bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Chill: Refrigerate the mixture for at least 1 hour to ensure it’s cold before churning.
  3. Churn: Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it thickens and reaches a soft-serve consistency (usually 20-25 minutes).
  4. Add-ins: If you’d like to add chocolate chips, fruit, or flavorings, add them in during the last few minutes of churning.
  5. Freeze: Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 3-4 hours, or until firm.
  6. Serve: Scoop out the ice cream and serve in bowls or cones. Enjoy!
