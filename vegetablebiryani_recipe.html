Recipe Details

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, potato, beans)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 green cardamom pods
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare Rice: Wash basmati rice thoroughly and soak for 30 minutes. Cook rice with water and a pinch of salt until 70% cooked. Drain the water and set aside.
  2. Cook Vegetables: Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods. Saute for a minute. Add onions and cook until golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes, vegetables, biryani masala, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook for 5-7 minutes until vegetables soften.
  3. Add Yogurt: Stir in the yogurt and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the mixture thickens slightly.
  4. Layer Biryani: In a large pot, layer cooked rice and vegetable mixture. Start with a layer of rice, then vegetables, and repeat. Add coriander and mint leaves between layers for extra flavor.
  5. Cook on Low Heat: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve: Garnish with more fresh coriander and mint leaves before serving hot with raita or salad.
