Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • 4-6 black tea bags
  • 4 cups boiling water
  • 1/4 cup sugar (or to taste, optional)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh lemon slices (optional, for garnish)
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Brew the Tea: In a heatproof container, pour 4 cups of boiling water over the tea bags. Let it steep for 5-7 minutes, depending on how strong you like your tea.
  2. Sweeten the Tea: If desired, stir in the sugar while the tea is still hot. Let the tea cool down to room temperature.
  3. Cool the Tea: Once the tea has cooled, add 4 cups of cold water to dilute it. Place it in the refrigerator to chill for at least an hour.
  4. Serve: Fill a glass with ice cubes and pour the chilled tea over the ice.
  5. Garnish and Enjoy: Garnish with fresh lemon slices and mint leaves for a refreshing touch.
