Grilled Chicken Wings

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 375°F/190°C).
  2. Season Wings: Toss chicken wings with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill: Arrange wings on the grill. Cook for 20-25 minutes, turning every 5 minutes until crispy and fully cooked.
  4. Optional Sauce: Brush with barbecue sauce during the last 5 minutes for added flavor.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with your favorite dip!
