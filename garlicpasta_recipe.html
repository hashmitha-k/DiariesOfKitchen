Creamy Garlic Pasta

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 5-6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook Pasta: Cook the pasta in salted boiling water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Garlic: Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the garlic for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Make Cream Sauce: Add butter and heavy cream to the pan. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
  4. Mix Pasta: Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to coat in the creamy sauce.
  5. Add Parmesan: Stir in the grated parmesan cheese and mix until the sauce thickens.
  6. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve: Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 550 kcal per serving
  • Protein: 15g
  • Fat: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Non-Vegan: No
  • Gluten-Free: No
  • Servings: 2
