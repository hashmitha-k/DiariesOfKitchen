Bruschetta

Home Recipes Favorites Cooking Tips
    

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of baguette or rustic Italian bread (sliced into 1-inch thick pieces)
  • 3-4 ripe tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil (chopped)
  • 2-3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: balsamic vinegar for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Tomato Topping: Start by dicing ripe tomatoes and placing them in a bowl. Add salt and let them sit for about 10 minutes to draw out excess moisture.
  2. Drain Excess Liquid: After the tomatoes have rested, drain the liquid to prevent the topping from becoming soggy.
  3. Mix Ingredients: Add minced garlic, freshly chopped basil, a generous drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, and freshly ground black pepper to the drained tomatoes. Stir gently to combine all the flavors. Let this mixture sit for at least 30 minutes for best taste.
  4. Prepare the Bread: Slice a rustic baguette or Italian loaf into 1-inch thick pieces. Lightly brush both sides with olive oil. Toast or grill the slices until they’re golden and crisp on the outside but still tender in the middle.
  5. Rub with Garlic: While the toasted bread is still warm, rub one side of each slice with a peeled clove of garlic for extra flavor.
  6. Assemble the Bruschetta: Spoon the tomato topping generously onto the garlic-rubbed side of each toasted slice.
  7. Serve: Enjoy immediately while the bread is warm and crispy! Optionally, drizzle with a touch of balsamic vinegar or reduction for added tanginess.

Dietary Information

  • Calories: 140 kcal per serving
  • Fat: 7g
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 17g
  • Gluten-Free: Yes (if using gluten-free bread)
  • Dairy-Free: Yes
  • Servings: 6-8
