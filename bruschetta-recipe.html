Recipe Details

Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of baguette or rustic Italian bread (sliced into 1-inch thick pieces)
  • 3-4 ripe tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil (chopped)
  • 2-3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: balsamic vinegar for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Bread: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the slices of bread on a baking sheet. Toast the bread slices in the oven for about 5-7 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. You can also grill the bread for a smokier flavor.
  2. Make the Tomato Mixture: While the bread is toasting, dice the tomatoes and chop the fresh basil. In a bowl, combine the tomatoes, basil, and minced garlic. Drizzle the olive oil over the mixture, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix everything gently to combine.
  3. Assemble the Bruschetta: Once the bread slices are toasted, rub each slice with a cut clove of garlic to add flavor. Spoon the tomato mixture generously over each toasted slice of bread.
  4. Optional: Drizzle a little balsamic vinegar over the top for an extra burst of flavor.
  5. Serve: Enjoy your homemade bruschetta as a tasty appetizer or snack!
