Pretzels

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup baking soda (for boiling water)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for brushing)
  • Coarse salt for topping

Instructions

  1. Activate Yeast: In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. Prepare Dough: Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Stir to combine and knead until you have a smooth dough (about 5-7 minutes).
  3. Let Dough Rise: Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.
  4. Shape the Pretzels: Once risen, punch down the dough and divide it into 8 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a rope and shape it into a pretzel.
  5. Boil the Pretzels: In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add baking soda. Gently drop each pretzel into the water for about 30 seconds, then remove and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Brush & Bake: Brush each pretzel with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse salt. Bake at 425째F (220째C) for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Serve: Let the pretzels cool for a few minutes, then serve warm with mustard or cheese sauce!
