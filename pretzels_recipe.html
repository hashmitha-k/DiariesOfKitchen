Pretzels

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup baking soda (for boiling water)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for brushing)
  • Coarse salt for topping

Instructions

  1. Activate yeast by mixing warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit until foamy.
  2. Mix flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into smooth dough. Let rise until doubled.
  3. Shape into pretzels. Boil in baking soda water for 30 seconds.
  4. Brush with egg, sprinkle salt, and bake at 425°F (220°C) for 12-15 minutes.

Dietary Information

  • Gluten-Free: No (contains flour)
  • Vegetarian: Yes
  • Contains Dairy: No
  • Contains Eggs: Yes (for brushing)
  • Vegan: No (contains egg)
